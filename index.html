Our services

Styling

Inside Collection for Summer 2018 presented by Jean Louis David portrays determined and independent women with proof that feminity comes in all guises throughout the day ...and night! Blonde bobs, all over loose curls and urban waves with exclusive contrasting techniques. Creative Director, Neil Smith for Eufora Editorial Team 2017 presents their latest collection 'Afterglow' A fabulous collection of hairstyles with movement and texture. “Modern Romance" collection created by Gary Taylor of Edward & Co expresses stunning, bold shapes with rich colours.

Coloring

Follow the newest hair color trends and get hair color ideas. Learn how to dye hair and then easily create your ideal look with temporary or permanent hair dye. Use our hair coloring tips to find the best way to cover grays, achieve the perfect ombré or simply pick a shade that's flawless and flattering.

Haircuts

Hair trends come and go each season, but there's a set of cuts that have proven to stand the test of time and thus, will always be in style. Need proof? Look no further than the red carpet. While your favorite celebs aren't afraid of dramatic changes, many stars have one haircut that they always return to. Whether you loyal to a long length, love a lob, or want to try a pixie, you're guaranteed to find your new signature cut thanks to the following timeless styles.
